# Wonder Trail Mobile Alpha Playtest Guide

## Purpose

This playtest checks whether the first mobile Alpha is understandable, comfortable, and inviting for children.

The main question is:

> Does the child want to continue helping 小航 after the first few stages?

## Device Scope

Test on:

- iPhone Safari
- Android Chrome

Use portrait orientation first.

## Setup

1. Open the game URL on the phone.
2. Turn off other distractions if possible.
3. Let the child hold the phone if safe.
4. Avoid explaining too much before the first stage.
5. Observe quietly and only help if the child is truly stuck.

## What To Observe

### First 30 Seconds

- Does the child notice 小航?
- Does the child understand this is an adventure?
- Does the child know where to tap?
- Does the child try to start without adult help?
- Does the child notice or use the speaker button?

### During Stages

- Are targets easy to see?
- Are objects easy to tap?
- Does the child understand the instruction text?
- Does the selected free system voice sound warm enough?
- Does immediate voice feedback feel fast enough?
- Does the voice talk too much or interrupt play?
- Does right-side Zhuyin help, distract, or feel neutral?
- Does the child use hints?
- Does the child get frustrated after wrong taps?

### Rewards

- Does the child react to stars?
- Does the child react to stickers?
- Does the child notice progress?
- Does the reward moment feel too fast, too slow, or right?

### Session End

- Does the child understand the story is finished for today?
- Does the child ask to continue?
- Does the child want to play again later?

## Success Signals

- Child plays at least 5 minutes in Alpha.
- Child completes at least 3 stages.
- Child understands the basic Search mechanic.
- Child recognizes or comments on 小航.
- Child wants to keep going or come back.

## Warning Signals

- Child cannot understand the first instruction.
- Child repeatedly taps but cannot hit objects.
- Child ignores 小航 completely.
- Child exits before stage 2.
- Child becomes visibly frustrated.
- Adult needs to explain every stage.

## Notes For Adults

Do not ask leading questions like:

```text
你是不是很喜歡小航？
```

Prefer neutral prompts:

```text
你看到什麼？
你覺得接下來要做什麼？
你想不想再玩一關？
```

## After Test Review

Record:

- Device used
- Age
- Session length
- Last completed stage
- Most confusing moment
- Most exciting moment
- Whether the child asked to continue
- Any adult help needed

Then compare with the in-game test record panel:

- Completed stages
- Wrong taps
- Hints
- Average seconds
